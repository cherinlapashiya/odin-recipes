Hydrabadi Lauki Kheer with Mini Rasgullas

Description

Hyderabadi Lauki Kheer with Mini Rasgullas recipe is a traditional Hyderabadi delicacy, which is served at weddings and other grand functions. This unique kheer is made with lauki but you will never be able to guess that when you taste it!

Ingridents

Steps

  1. Heat the milk
  2. Add grated bottle guard
  3. Add dry ftuit
  4. Cool it and serve chilled
